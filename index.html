Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to the most common questions from international students.

What are the specific entry requirements for Adelaide University?

expand_more

Entry requirements are tiered based on your chosen program. For undergraduates, successful completion of a recognized secondary qualification (like A-Levels, IB, or ATAR) is required. Postgraduates need a bachelor's degree from a recognized institution with a minimum GPA. English proficiency (IELTS 6.5+ or equivalent) is mandatory for most programs.

What student support services are available on campus?

expand_more

We provide comprehensive support including:
• Career Services: Job placement, internship matching, and resume workshops.
• Accommodation: Assistance with university-managed housing and private rentals.
• Health & Wellbeing: On-campus medical clinics, 24/7 counseling, and fitness centers.

Are there scholarships available for international students?

expand_more

Yes! Adelaide University offers several scholarships, including the Global Citizens Scholarship, which can reduce tuition fees by 15% to 30%. PFEC Global can also help you identify exclusive bursaries.

How can PFEC Global help with my application?

expand_more

PFEC Global provides end-to-end support, including course selection advice, document certification, application submission, visa processing assistance, and pre-departure briefings—all free of charge for students.
